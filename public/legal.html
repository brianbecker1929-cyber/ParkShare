ParkShare uses Stripe, Inc. to process all payments. ParkShare is not a bank and does not directly hold or store your payment card details.

2. How Charges Work (Renters)

Your total charge is calculated as (hourly rate × hours booked) + service fee, and charged in full via Stripe Checkout at booking confirmation. You'll receive a receipt via email.

3. Service Fees

ParkShare charges a service fee, shown at checkout, on each booking. Service fees are non-refundable except where a booking is cancelled or refunded per Section 5.

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. If a Renter disputes a charge with their bank ("chargeback"), Hosts may have payouts withheld or reversed if the dispute is upheld.
